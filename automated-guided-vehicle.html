Bottle Unscrambler

Fully automatic plastic Bottle Unscrambler with a pre-feeding system, suitable for all kinds of rigid plastic containers of various shapes and sizes.

Bottle / Can Depalletizer

Auto depalletizing of empty containers (PET, Glass or Can) and inline feeding to the filling machine.

Bottle Rinsers

Rotary Rinsing Machines are suitable for cleaning of rigid containers (PET, HDPE, Glass) before the Filling Operation.

Filling & Capping

Linear and Rotary Rinsing, Filling & Capping solutions using Net weight, Flow meter and Level filling technologies. Rotary cappers for various types of closures.

Labelling

Roll feed, Hot melt, Cold glue, Self-adhesive and shrink sleeve labelling machines in standalone or combined versions for varied shaped bottles.

Pouch, Sachet & StickPack

Form Fill Seal Solution for packaging of granulated, compressed, liquid, pasty and solid products in variety of flexible packages.

Overwrapping Machines

Carton Overwrapping machines ranging from low cost entry level machinery to high speed integrated systems to wrap products for presentation, multi-packing & distribution purpose.

CW, X RAY, VISION Inspection

Monitoring and analysis solution for critical quality parameters via Vision, X-ray, Metal and Check Weighing detection.

Polyolefin Films (POF)

Shrink Wrapping with POF Film

Shrink Bundling with LDPE film

Automated Guided Vehicles

Clearpack has partnered with Guangdong Jaten Robot & Automation Co., Ltd, often referred to as “Jaten” for Automated Guided Vehicles solution for internal warehouse logistics. AGVs are ideal for repetitive tasks under conditions where humans don’t operate with full efficiency like:

  • Conveyor to conveyor material transfer
  • Ground to ground pallet transfer
  • Ground to height pallet transfer
  • Trolley tugging type application
  • Trolley lurking type application
  • Unit load application
