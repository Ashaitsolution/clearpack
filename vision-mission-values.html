Vision

We are an awesome organization, with global presence and leadership. Customer Delight is our top most Corporate Goal. We care for all our stakeholders and enjoy healthy growth and profitability. We are a responsible corporate citizen.

Mission

We provide optimum packaging solutions and services that reflect our leadership in the industry and deliver excellent values to our customers, employees, business partners and share holders.

Values
  • Teamwork - We support and encourage teamwork and personal development to ensure a high level of competence, expertise and satisfaction.
  • Honesty and Integrity - Impeccable ethics and morals will be the cornerstone of all our interactions and relationships. We will consistently demonstrate principled & ethical judgment and practice the highest standards of social & professional conduct.
  • Excellence with Continuous Improvement - An environment that promotes excellence in performance, accountability, mutual respect, trust, cooperation and fairness.
  • Growth and Profitability - We continuously seek opportunities for profitable growth for all our stakeholders.
  • Build Trust by Keeping Commitments - We take accountability for delivering on our commitments. We believe in winning trust of our internal and external customers by achieving mutually agreed goals even when it's tough and demonstrate the company's dedication to live by its values.
