A Successful Packtech 2014, Mumbai – India

Clearpack was a part of the recently concluded Packtech 2014 at Mumbai jointly held with Drinktech 2014. The show saw good amount of visitors from various quarters of the packaging world and was a great opportunity for Clearpack to showcase its solutions in Primary Packaging, Secondary packaging, Case Packing & Shrink Wrapping, Palletizers and complete Turnkey Lines.

Clearpack booth was meticulously planned and used corporate colours of RED and VIOLET to make it look both lively and modern. With video screens, posters, banners, brochures & samples we covered the full range of applications from our core product lines.

A large number of visitors to the booth provided an opportunity for Clearpack to showcase our world class packaging solutions, while establishing our brand presence.

Participation in Packtech was a very successful one with new contacts being established & the brand equity as a packaging leader in Asia getting a further boost.

We thank all our esteemed visitors for making the show a success.
