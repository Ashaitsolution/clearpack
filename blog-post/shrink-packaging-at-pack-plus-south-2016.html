Shrink Packaging at Pack Plus South 2016

Shrink Packaging Systems Private Limited would like to thank all visitors at recently concluded 6th edition of PackPlus South & SupplyPlus South 2016 at HITEX Exhibition Centre, Hyderabad. The exhibition, held from 2-5 March 2016, witnessed a footfall of 7552 visitors with high interest in packaging, converting, processing and supply chain machineries.

The visitors could have a look and feel at the latest developments of our range of machines. Quick sampling was offered to select visitors at our booth. Shrink Packaging Systems Private Limited displayed Shrink Packaging Solutions for Presentation and Transit Applications along with Shrink Films:

Presentation Application

  • LHS 115 a Semi- Automatic Shrink Wrapper with Shrink Tunnel
  • EFK 250 an Automatic Shrink Wrapper with Shrink Tunnel

Transit Application

  • AVW 65 an Automatic Sleeve Wrapper with Shrink Tunnel

Shrink Films

  • POF Films
  • LDPE Films

Shrink Wrapping Solutions for Presentation Application plays an extremely important role to offer your brand a presentable look in a highly competitive shelf space in retailing. This presentation makes up for a tamper-evident pack and sparkling, glossy appearance too.

Shrink Packaging Solution for Transit Application helps you to collate products such as jars, tins, bottles etc. together using a strong film or with a cardboard tray, whilst in transit. Apart from protecting the product, this packaging significantly lowers your packing costs when you switch from corrugated cartons. Automatic machines with collation also result in saving on labour & deliver higher efficiency.

Consumables such as Shrink Films were offered mainly in two varieties- POF Shrink Films, LDPE Films. POF Films are widely used to improve presentation of your brand/ product. While LDPE Films are available in two variants viz., Shrink Films & Stretch Films. LDPE Shrink Films are used for bundling your products for transit while LDPE Stretch Films are used to Stretch Wrap the pallets/ boxes for transportation.
