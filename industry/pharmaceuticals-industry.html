PHARMACEUTICALS

Clearpack provides wide range of safe, hygienic, and cost-effective packaging machine solutions for pharmaceutical industry. This includes Form-Fill-Seal machines for flexible packaging formats such as Sachets and Stickpacks for powder, granules or pasty liquid and rigid container solutions such as Container Feeding, Air Rinsing, Filling, Capping, and Labelling for a liquid application like syrups and sanitizers. Our machines can shrink pack various products such as glass, PET, boxes, trays, jars, cartons of assorted sizes in a wide range of product collations.

Our Compact Case packing and Palletizing system with Collaborative and Industrial robots for light weight pharmaceuticals products is one of the most sought economical end-of-line packaging solution in our portfolio for this industry. Integration with vision and track and trace systems can be provided. Individual packaging machines as well as complete packaging line can be delivered in Industry 4.0 ready conditions.
